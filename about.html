About Me

I am currently a Masters Student in the Graduate School of Information at the University of California Berkeley. My interests are improving workplace information systems to better enhance collaboration and knowledge sharing. I also went to CAL for my undergraduate degrees in Computer Science and Business Administration. Go Bears!

Prior to graduate school, I worked for six years in the Seattle Puget Sound for Microsoft. I was part of the enterprise sales and marketing team for the Microsoft Information Worker Division and the Windows Server Division.

As a Bay Area native, I can:

  • Tell you the exact place I was during the Loma Prieta Earthquake
  • Remember when all of the East Bay was still 510
  • Correct you when you say anything else except Candlestick Park
  • Recall seeing the smoke from the Oakland Hills fire
  • Reminisce about the Exploratorium when it was in the Palace of Fine Arts
